MOLE PASTE & CHILE PASTES

  • Mole Paste.
  • Mole paste is a mixture of chili , chocolate , sugar , peanuts and spices, is a Mexican condiment to prepare dishes such as enchiladas with mole, mole chicken , turkey with mole, pork ribs with mole, etc. The method of preparation is to put 3 cups of water per cup of mole paste in a pot, then heat the mixture until you get the desired consistency.
  • Chile Paste.
  • Chile pastes ideal for seasoning dishes, prepare marinades, sauces, dips, stews, soups and broths season, desserts made from dried chilies and spices
