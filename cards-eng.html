ABOUT US

We have focused since the year of 2003 on giving to our clients new options for their development in areas such as: efficiency, reducing losses, and facilitating the storage of their restaurants and kitchens.

We have tried to share with Mexicans that live in the USA the authentic flavor of the Mexican cuisine. Our products not only contain the traditional Mexican flavor, they also brings memories from home.

We create innovative products that give our clients the opportunity to have a wide variety of flavors and recipes in their offer. Our products are essential to give that traditional taste to the basic Mexican dishes.

The Aarmes team is formed by a group of professionals with more than 25 years of experience in the alimentary industry.
